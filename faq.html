Frequently Asked Questions

How often do you post new content? ▼
I strive to post new content 2-3 times per week, typically on Mondays, Wednesdays, and Fridays. Content includes:
  • In-depth product reviews of the latest beauty releases
  • Step-by-step makeup tutorials for various occasions
  • Skincare routines and tips based on different skin types
  • Seasonal makeup trends and how to incorporate them
For exclusive updates and early access to new content, subscribe to my newsletter or follow me on Instagram and Facebook.
Do you accept product reviews? ▼
Yes, I do accept products for review! My approach to reviews is:
  • 100% honest and transparent feedback based on my personal experience
  • I maintain full editorial control over all content
  • I only accept products that align with my audience's interests
  • Each review includes pros, cons, and who the product would work best for
  • I typically test products for 2-4 weeks before reviewing
If you're interested in sending products for review, please contact me through the contact form with details about your product and brand. I respond to all inquiries within 2-3 business days.
How can I contact you? ▼
You can reach me through several channels:
  • Email: contact@makeupbyny.com (response within 24-48 hours)
  • Social Media: Direct message me on Instagram or Facebook
  • Contact Form: Use the contact form on the website for general inquiries, collaboration requests, or feedback
  • Booking: For makeup services and appointments, use my booking page
I personally read and respond to all messages, though response times may vary during busy periods or holidays.
Do you offer makeup tutorials? ▼
Absolutely! Tutorials are a core part of my content. My tutorial offerings include:
  • Written Tutorials: Detailed step-by-step guides with product recommendations
  • Video Tutorials: Available on my YouTube channel and embedded in blog posts
  • Beginner-Friendly Guides: Perfect for those just starting their makeup journey
  • Advanced Techniques: For makeup enthusiasts looking to expand their skills
  • Seasonal Looks: Special occasion makeup for holidays, events, and seasons
I also offer personalized one-on-one virtual makeup lessons for those who want customized guidance. These can be booked through my booking page.
Can I use your photos on my website? ▼
All content on Makeup by NY (including photos, videos, text, and graphics) is protected by copyright. Here's my policy on using my content:
  • Personal Use: You may save images for personal, non-commercial inspiration
  • Social Media Sharing: You may share my content on social media platforms with proper credit (@_ryymone on Instagram)
  • Commercial Use: Prior written permission is required for any commercial use
  • Blogs/Websites: If you'd like to feature my work, please contact me first
When permission is granted, proper attribution must include:
  1. Credit to "Makeup by NY" or "Nyisha Rymone"
  2. A link back to my website (makeupbyny.com)
  3. No alterations to the original content without approval
To request permission, please use the contact form with details about how you intend to use the content.
