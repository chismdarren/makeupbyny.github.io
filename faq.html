Frequently Asked Questions

How often do you post new content? ▼
I typically post new content 2-3 times per week. This includes product reviews, tutorials, and beauty tips. You can subscribe to my newsletter or follow me on social media to stay updated with new posts.
Do you accept product reviews? ▼
Yes, I do accept product reviews! However, I maintain full editorial control and only review products that I genuinely believe in. All reviews are honest and based on my personal experience. If you're interested in sending products for review, please contact me through the contact form.
How can I contact you? ▼
You can reach me through various channels:
  • Email: contact@makeupbyny.com
  • Social Media: Follow me on Instagram and Facebook
  • Contact Form: Use the contact form on the website
Do you offer makeup tutorials? ▼
Yes! I regularly post makeup tutorials on my blog and YouTube channel. These tutorials range from everyday looks to special occasion makeup. You can find all tutorials in the "Tutorials" section of the blog.
Can I use your photos on my website? ▼
All photos on this website are protected by copyright. If you'd like to use any of my photos, please contact me for permission. When granted, proper attribution and a link back to my website are required.
